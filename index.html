Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov is a mixed martial artist, the Champion of the Eurasian Cup of Hand-to-Hand Combat and the finalist of the Russian Championship. He is a two-time Combat Sambo World Champion..

Khabib Abdulmanapovich Nurmagomedov competed in the lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship, where he was the longest-reigning UFC Lightweight Champion, having held the title from April 2018 to March 2021.
